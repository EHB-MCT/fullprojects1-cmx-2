Lubnan Al Wazny

Volunteer

"I came from Iraq in 2015, and that’s when I met Gwendoline and Johan, the coordinators of Cinemaximiliaan. The same day, I met them in the Cinemaximiliaan park near the commissariat. I reached out to them, and they invited me to join the Cinemaximiliaan project. Cinemaximiliaan is now my home, or my second home, because my real home is in Iraq. Here, Cinemaximiliaan is my family, and we are a really big family. "​

Cinemaximiliaan in pictures

Under an improvised tent canvas, we bring cinema to the people, a typical example of our mobile and accessible Screenings.

We organize cultural trips and workshops to strengthen the ties between different communities and to promote cultural exchange.

Cinemaximiliaan brings people together through film and culture. Our Events offer a place where newcomers and locals meet and share stories.

Cinemaximiliaan encourages creative expression. With film workshops productions, we give newcomers a voice to tell their stories. tell.

At this open-air film screening we create a warm and cozy atmosphere, where people come together and film as a connecting element experienced.
